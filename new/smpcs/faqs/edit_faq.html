Note
  • To make a text

    sub-heading

    , just enclose the text in <h4></h4>. For eg. - <h4>A sub heading</h4>
  • To make a text bold, just enclose the text in <b></b>. For eg. - <b>Some text in bold</b>
  • To make a text underline, just enclose the text in <u></u>. For eg. - <u>Some text underlined</u>
  • To make a text italic, just enclose the text in <i></i>. For eg. - <i>Some text in italic</i>
  • To add a link to text, just enclose the text in <a href='full_url'></a>. For eg. - <a href='https://www.google.com'>Visit Google</i>
